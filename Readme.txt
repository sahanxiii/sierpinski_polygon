demoFractalPolygon draws out a regular polygon with n sides. (User chooses n)
Then it will draw out a sierpinski triangle on top of each side.
User chooses the depth of the triangle too.

demoInverted draws out a regular polygon with n sides.
Then it will draw out a sierpinski triangle inside the polygon.
User chooses the depth of the triangle.
